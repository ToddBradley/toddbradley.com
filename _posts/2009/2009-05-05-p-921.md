## [Sugar Stacks - How Much Sugar Is in That?]({% post_url 2009/2009-05-05-p-921 %})


Next time you give your kid a box of raisins because it’s supposedly a “healthy” snack, consider how much sugar is concentrated in those dried raisins.


[![](/assets/img/converted/raisins.jpg)](http://sugarstacks.com/)


[SugarStacks.com](http://sugarstacks.com/) is a website that shows the amount of sugar contained in every day type foods, represented by sugar cubes.  That photo above explains why [Dr. Fuhrman](http://www.drfuhrman.com/)’s “Eat to Live” diet forbids dried fruit, which I always assumed to be nutritious.
