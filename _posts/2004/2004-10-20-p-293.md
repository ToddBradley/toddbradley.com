[BrainTenYrs.mov (video/quicktime Object)](http://movies10.archive.org/3/movies/BrainTenYrs/BrainTenYrs.mov) - Here’s a QuickTime movie that compares Dubya’s debating skills 10 years ago to his skill level now. I’m sure the folks who put this together have an axe to grind, but it’s pretty interesting to see that when he was debating to become governor of Texas, he actually seemed like an intelligent, thoughtful man who could make an articulate point. What happened in the past 10 years?
