## [my first Mac, part 2]({% post_url 2006/2006-03-12-p-506 %})


Well, I spent Saturday morning clearing stuff out of my studio and getting it ready for sale on eBay.  That was to create the room for my PowerMac setup.  So, in the afternoon I finally got around to setting the thing up.  I upgraded to the latest OS version, configured it all how I think I’ll like it, and installed Soundtrack Pro on it.  Then I put on Firefox and Bittorrent.  One thing’s for sure: the one-button mouse that was included in the package is just not going to cut it.  So, next up is to set up a cordless mouse with two or more buttons and a scroll wheel.
