Ever heard of DNS-over-HTTPS? I hadn’t, but the engineers at Firefox have been working hard to implement it in their web browser. It’s another way they’re fighting the good fight to make the web secure.Here is their page explaining how it works: [https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/firefox-dns-over-https](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/firefox-dns-over-https)If you use Firefox and live in the US, this will be enabled automatically for you. And if you don’t use Firefox, you should.
