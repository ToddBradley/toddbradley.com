Two months after Brooke and I [got married in the mountains]({% post_url 2023/2023-08-02-p-3445 %}) near Durango, we threw a party in Denver to celebrate with family and friends. Instead of a traditional wedding reception with dinner, a DJ, speeches, etc. we had an intimate cocktail wedding reception with open bar and a tiny selection of finger foods. The location was divine, at the [FIRE Restaurant & Lounge](https://www.thearthotel.com/dine-drink/fire) on the roof of the ART Hotel in Denver.


Brooke and I took a few photos on our phones. But fortunately several guests also took pictures and shared them with us. Here are my favorites. Thank you to our photographers Rebecca, Matt, Amy, Tom, and Jen. And thank you to everyone else who showed up to help us celebrate our wedding, especially those who traveled from out of town.
